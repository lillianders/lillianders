My Work

+Limb: Design and Evaluation of a Lower Limb Orthoprosthesis for Toddlers with Fibular Hemimelia Using Additive Manufacturing

This project represents my Master Thesis work, where I developed and clinically evaluated a modular 3D-printed orthoprosthesis for toddlers with Fibular Hemimelia. The research focused on improving mobility and gait symmetry through user-centered design and comprehensive sensor analysis. The work demonstrates the feasibility and effectiveness of additive manufacturing in pediatric orthoprosthetics, providing a personalized, cost-effective solution for young patients.

This work was completed as my Master Thesis and has been submitted as a peer-reviewed paper for publication.
